Nastassia Grimm

PhD Candidate in Theoretical Cosmology

Center for Theoretical Astrophysics and Cosmology, University of Zurich

I'm a PhD student in Jaiyul Yoo's group at the Center for Theoretical Astrophysics and Cosmology, University of Zurich. My work is focused on general relativistic effects in cosmological observables, such as the galaxy number density, the luminosity distance and cosmic shear. I am also interested in a variety of other fields in astrophysics and cosmology, such as modified gravity, gravitational waves, inflationary physics, and applications of machine learning.

On this website, you can find more information about my research and publications, my CV and of course my contact information. I'm always happy to hear from you!
